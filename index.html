Notes

  • Bolted cover-plate splice connecting two beam sections.
  • Based on SCI P398 "Joints in Steel Construction — Moment Connections" Example D.1.
  • Carries section moment and shear capacity.
  • Bolt slip resistance checked against SLS only.
  • Splice is near a restraint so does not carry moments due to strut action.
  • Flange and web splice are using same bolt diameter.
